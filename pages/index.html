HELPING THE DROPOUTS

Giving hope for high school dropouts in Kenya

About the service

One of the major problems experienced at many high schools currently in Kenya is on dropout cases due to various reasons such as teenage pregnancy, early marriages, child labour and so on.


Therefore, the aim of this service is in tracking those who have dropped out so as to be used as a means to help them to return to school. Scroll down to get more details on the service.

Features

The school administration (after adding the current students and students who are beginning their high school journey) can view the students' data for the current year and see which student(s) is available and which ones are not available(left the school). In addition, the NGO side can view details of dropout students (those who were not available and did not return or transfer to another school within a month) and the the returnees (those who were dropouts but have successfully returned to school).

The school administration can search for a student who has transferred from a list of all students in certain region and other regions or from dropouts list and automatically add him or her to their own school records.

The NGO side can view statistical analysis of number of dropouts and returnees overall, per gender, per region and over time. This is useful for creating reports on quantitative analysis on dropout rates, useful for NGO and government in making decisions on the subject matter.
